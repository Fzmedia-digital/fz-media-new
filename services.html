How does the video files transfer process work?

It is simple! Once you order, you can upload raw footage folders directly through our Client Portal, or paste Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer links. We handle the rest!

What is your delivery timeframe?

Standard packages are delivered within 2 to 4 business days. For urgent campaigns, we offer expedited 24-hour turnaround speeds in the higher package configurations.

Can I request changes or modifications?

Yes! Each pricing tier includes dedicated iterations. In our Client Dashboard, you can pause any video draft frame-by-frame, type your suggestions, and we will update it immediately.
